About Me

Hi, I'm Toprak!

I'm 15 years old and currently in high school. Ever since I was 9, I've had a strong passion for coding and technology. What started as a curiosity has grown into a love for building interactive projects, solving problems, and exploring new ways to make things work.

Over the years, I've created several games and websites, and I'm always working on new ideas and improving my skills. Some of the projects I'm most proud of include:

  • Qalculator - A calculator with a fun, interactive twist.
  • Typamelody - A game that challenges you to type faster to the rhythm of music.
  • NationalityGuessr - A game where you guess the nationality of various people.

My coding journey has been incredibly rewarding, and I love sharing my projects with others. Whether it's solving a bug or creating a new feature, there's always something exciting to learn and improve.

I look forward to the future and can't wait to continue creating and learning more in the world of programming. Thanks for visiting my page!
